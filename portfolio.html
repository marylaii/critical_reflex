DIR://PRIVATE_DIVISION/KSP2/INSIDERS_EVENT
PREVIEW EVENT AT EUROPEAN SPACE AGENCY FACILITIES
Organized a preview event for Kerbal Space Program 2's Early Access launch. Invited key journalists and content creators from USA, EMEA and Japan to ESA facilities in Noordwijk, Netherlands. Designed as an immersive event that put guests inside the actual world KSP2 simulates.

Apart from the play session, special events included: a mission design workshop at ESA, retro arcades post-event chill, and a private "dinner under the stars" at a planetarium, which was timed to coincide with a real SpaceX Falcon 9 launch happening that same day, watched live by the group on the dome of the building.

03 // EXCLUSIVE INTERVIEW - DISPATCH x IGN ART DIRECTOR FEATURE
03
03 Dispatch - Exclusive Character Art Interview with IGN DIR://ICO/ADHOC_STUDIO/DISPATCH/ART_DIRECTOR_INTERVIEW
SPECIAL FEATURE // DISPATCH x IGN - NOV 2025 ◼ MISSION COMPLETE
DIR://ICO/ADHOC_STUDIO/DISPATCH/ART_DIRECTOR_INTERVIEW
Dispatch - special interview for IGN GOING VIRAL
Subject noticed the Dispatch community was actively debating what the game's hero designs reminded them of, with some accusing developers of copying other IPs (e.g., Malevola looking like Karlach). She decided to turn discussion into a story and arrange an exclusive interview with IGN to have Art Director Derek Stratton walk through the inspiration behind every single character design.

The interview became a short video content series across YT Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram. All videos got hundreds of thousands of views. Viral highlight: Derek explained that inspiration for Malevola was influencer LeanBeefPatty who, upon learning this, cosplayed the character irl. The resulting post hit 5.7M views on Instagram only.

05 Pitched Ideas - Marketing and Pre-Production STATUS://UNEXECUTED (SADLY)
Over 6 years in video game marketing, subject generated dozens of out-of-the-box ideas for the games. Most were sadly terminated at budgeting stage. The following ideas were developed as part of campaign planning for ONE BTN BOSSES, LAN PARTY ADVENTURES, and SMITE 2, among dozens of other ideas for various projects.
One BIG Btn Bosses
One Btn Bosses
In this game, player needs only one button to play. Create a custom huge button to play the game with. Send it to creators and media as part of the press kit, and deploy at trade shows. Tactile gimmick, instantly photographable and self-explanatory.
Dev Diaries as 2000s-style video news
LAN Party Adventures
A recurring TikTok/social series styled after Computer Chronicles, presenting dev diaries and game updates as if they're 2000s TV gaming news segments. Embrace bad quality and cringe. Provoke nostalgia.
Ex-Gods Play Their Characters
SMITE 2
Approach actors who have played mythological Gods in movies and TV shows and film them playing SMITE 2 as their own God character. Growing awareness through the fun concept, actor's audience, and extended media interest.
